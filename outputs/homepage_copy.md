## About Section (Below the Fold)

### Who We Are

Bloom & Brew started with a simple question: *why does great coffee so rarely come with great food?*

Founded in 2021 by two Bangalore natives — a specialty coffee roaster and a sourdough baker — we set out to build a space where neither takes a back seat. Our beans arrive green from single estates across Karnataka's Western Ghats, roasted in micro-batches three times a week at our in-house roastery. Our kitchen runs the same philosophy: small batches, real ingredients, nothing premade.

We're not a chain. We don't have a corporate menu. Every cup and plate reflects what's fresh, what's in season, and what we'd want to eat ourselves.

### The Space

Designed for people who like to linger. Deep couches for weekend reads. High tables for laptop mornings. A garden courtyard for conversations that deserve sunlight. Fast Wi-Fi, plenty of outlets, and a noise level that lets you think.

### Our Promise

- ☕ **Roasted fresh** — Beans roasted in-house, never older than 10 days
- 🌾 **Scratch-made** — Every dish prepared from whole ingredients, daily
- 🌿 **Locally sourced** — Produce from farms within 100km of Bangalore
- 📶 **Work-friendly** — High-speed Wi-Fi, power at every seat, no side-eye for staying all day
