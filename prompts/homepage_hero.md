# 🏠 Prompt Template: Homepage Hero Section

## Purpose
Generate a compelling homepage hero section with a clear value proposition, sub-headline, and introductory paragraph for a local business.

---

## Prompt Template

```
You are a senior conversion copywriter who specializes in local business websites. You write copy that is clear, benefit-driven, and optimized for turning visitors into customers. You avoid generic marketing fluff and clichés like "best in class," "one-stop shop," or "world-class service."

**Client Brief:**
- Business Name: {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
- Business Type: {{BUSINESS_TYPE}}
- Location: {{LOCATION}}
- Target Audience: {{TARGET_AUDIENCE}}
- Core USPs (Unique Selling Points): {{USPS}}
- Tone: {{TONE}} (choose from: friendly, professional, confident)
- Competitor Differentiation: {{DIFFERENTIATOR}}

**Task:**
Generate the homepage hero section with the following structure:

1. **Headline** (max 10 words)
   - Must communicate the core value proposition
   - Should create curiosity or emotional pull
   - Must NOT be generic (e.g., "Welcome to our business")

2. **Sub-headline** (max 20 words)
   - Clarify WHO this is for + the KEY BENEFIT they'll get
   - Use specific language related to the business type

3. **Intro Paragraph** (40–60 words)
   - Expand on the value proposition
   - Include the location naturally
   - End with a soft transition toward exploring the site

4. **Hero CTA Button Text** (2–5 words)
   - Action-oriented, specific to the business
   - NOT generic (avoid "Learn More" or "Click Here")

**Constraints:**
- Write at a Grade 8 reading level
- Use active voice only
- No exclamation marks in headlines
- Include at least one sensory word (taste, feel, aroma, warmth, etc.)
- Reference the specific location/neighborhood naturally

**Output Format:**
Return the copy in clearly labeled sections.
```

## Example: Bloom & Brew Café, Koramangala

### Variables Used:
```
BUSINESS_NAME: Bloom & Brew Café
BUSINESS_TYPE: Specialty coffee and artisan brunch café
LOCATION: Koramangala 5th Block, Bangalore
TARGET_AUDIENCE: Young professionals (25–35), remote workers, weekend brunch lovers
USPS: Single-origin pour-over coffee, sourdough-based brunch menu, co-working-friendly space with fast Wi-Fi
TONE: friendly
DIFFERENTIATOR: Not a chain — every cup is hand-brewed, every dish is made from scratch with locally sourced ingredients
```
