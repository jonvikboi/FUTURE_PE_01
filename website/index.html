Our Story

Made From Scratch
Since 2021

Three years, 47,000 cups of pour-over, and one very well-fed sourdough starter.

Bloom & Brew started with a simple question: why does great coffee so rarely come with great food?

Founded by two Bangalore natives — a specialty coffee roaster and a sourdough baker — we set out to build a space where neither takes a back seat. Our beans arrive green from single estates across Karnataka's Western Ghats, roasted in micro-batches three times a week at our in-house roastery.

We're not a chain. We don't have a corporate menu. Every cup and plate reflects what's fresh, what's in season, and what we'd want to eat ourselves.

What We Offer

Our Menu & Experiences

From single-origin pour-overs to scratch-made brunch plates — everything is crafted with intention.

Signature

Single-Origin Pour-Over

One farm, one cup, all the flavor.

Each pour-over begins with beans from a single estate in Coorg or Chikmagalur, roasted at our in-house micro-roastery. Your barista brews it by hand, adjusting grind and temperature to bring out each bean's natural tasting notes.

  • 3–4 rotating single-origin beans weekly
  • Hario V60, Chemex, or AeroPress — your pick
  • Tasting card with farm, altitude, and flavor profile
  • Beans roasted within 10 days — guaranteed
  • Buy 250g bags of any bean you try
We roast in-house and source directly — your barista can tell you the farmer's name.
Kitchen

Artisan Brunch Plates

Brunch that earns the wait.

Our brunch menu rotates with the seasons, built around what arrives fresh from farms within 100km. Think sourdough bases, free-range eggs from Hesaraghatta, ricotta we make every morning, and house-fermented hot sauces.

  • Seasonal menu updated monthly (4–6 plates)
  • 72-hour ferment sourdough baked fresh daily
  • Free-range eggs, house-made ricotta
  • Vegetarian & vegan options always available
  • Weekend brunch served until 3 PM
Every ingredient has a source we can name, every plate is made from scratch.
Bakery

Sourdough Bakery Selection

72 hours of patience in every loaf.

Our sourdough starts with a 4-year-old mother culture and stone-ground flour from Tumkur. The dough ferments for 72 hours before baking — no shortcuts, no commercial yeast, no preservatives.

  • Classic country sourdough (whole or half)
  • Rosemary-garlic focaccia (weekends only)
  • Seasonal specials: fig & walnut, olive & thyme
  • All loaves baked between 6–8 AM daily
  • Pre-order for guaranteed availability
We mill our own flour, use a 4-year-old starter — actual craft baking 10 feet from your table.

Events

Private Event Hosting

Good conversations deserve good settings.

Our garden courtyard and mezzanine level are available for private bookings — from team off-sites and book club meets to birthday brunches and coffee-tasting workshops. We handle the menu, the setup, and the playlist.

  • Custom food & beverage menu
  • Basic décor setup (florals, candles, settings)
  • Dedicated staff for the event duration
  • Add-on: coffee-tasting or latte-art workshop
Not a banquet hall — a café that opens its best corners for your people, with a curated menu.
